---
description: Analyzing data in the Reports page
---

1.  **Navigate to Reports**: access the Reports page from the sidebar menu.
2.  **View Current Month**: The dashboard defaults to the current month's data.
3.  **KPI Cards**: Review the total pending, completed, cancelled, and total jobs for the selected month.
4.  **Monthly Day-by-Day Chart**: The area chart visualizes the volume of shipments (daily count) for each day of the selected month.
5.  **Job Details Table**: A table at the bottom lists all individual jobs for the month, including their status, consignee, and port.
6.  **Change Month**: Use the arrows (`<` and `>`) next to the month name in the header to navigate to previous or future months.
